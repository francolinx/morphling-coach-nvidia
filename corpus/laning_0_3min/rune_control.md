# Rune Control (0:00 - 3:00)

## 0:00 bounty rune
Mid player usually goes top bounty (radiant) or bottom bounty (dire). Morphling
specifically has Waveform escape - you can contest more aggressively than
Invoker or OD. If you see only a squishy mid opponent at the bounty, take it.
If a strength offlaner is also there, concede and walk to lane.

## 2:00 first power runes
Prioritize Haste or Double Damage if it is safe to contest. Regeneration is
great because of your bottle. Arcane is low value for Morphling early -
you want Treads, not mana sustain. Illusion rune is a soft trap: it can be
used to fake a commit or to push a wave safely, but do not chase kills with it.

## Control risk
If you see enemy mid missing at 1:50, assume they are going for the 2:00 rune.
Pull back toward tower. Do not contest if HP is below 60%. The rune is worth
100-200 gold; a death is worth 300 gold and two minutes off the map.

## Bottling etiquette
The bottle holds three charges. Always refill to full before committing forward
for a last hit at the ranged creep. A half-empty bottle is a gank that you
lose.
