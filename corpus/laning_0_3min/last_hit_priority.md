# Morphling Last-Hit Priority

Morphling has a projectile attack with ~1900 ms projectile speed, which means
you need to start attack animation slightly earlier than instant-attack heroes.
If you right-click when the creep is at exactly the right HP, the projectile
arrives too late and the melee creep steals the hit.

## Creep priority under enemy tower
- Do not push the wave aggressively in the first 3 minutes. You have no real
  escape beyond Waveform (cooldown 10s at level 1, 4s at level 4).
- Aim to last-hit from behind your own creep line. This keeps you in range of
  your tower for emergency pull-back.
- Ranged creep last hits are easiest - start your attack when it has roughly
  80 HP remaining so the projectile lands at zero.

## Deny priority
- Your base damage is high enough to deny reliably. Aim for 8-12 denies by
  3 minutes.
- Denies also reduce enemy mid XP gain - doubly valuable against experience-
  dependent heroes like Invoker or OD.

## Wave management in the first 3 minutes
Hold the wave at your side of the river. Morphling farms best in a slightly-
pushed-toward-you wave because you can collect bottle refills from rune spawns
without exposing yourself. A pushed wave puts you visible to both side lanes
for ganks; a held wave keeps you safe and on equal CS.
