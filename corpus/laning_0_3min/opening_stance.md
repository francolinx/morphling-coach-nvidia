# Morphling Opening Stance (0:00 - 3:00)

## Starting items (mid)
Tango, Healing Salve, Ironwood Branch x2, Faerie Fire, Mango. This gives you
enough regen to survive two magical trades and one all-in from the offlane duo.

## Innate: Essence Shift (active from level 1, no skill point needed)
As of 7.41, Essence Shift is Morphling's INNATE ability. It is active from
the moment the game starts — every right-click on an enemy hero permanently
steals agility (3/5/7/9 per hit, scaling with hero level, not ability
level; base duration 10s after the 7.41b nerf). You do not skill it and
cannot skip it.

Trading implication from minute 0: every right-click you land on the enemy
laner is permanent agility for you and a temporary agility loss for them.
This is especially strong against melee offlaners who must stand close to
last-hit — they cannot avoid eating right-clicks and they bleed stats.
Look for short trade windows where you can land 2-3 hits before backing
off; the stack value compounds over the lane.

## Level 1 ability pick
Because Essence Shift is innate, your level 1 skill point is a free choice
between Waveform, Adaptive Strike, and Attribute Shift. Take Waveform (Q)
for mobility AND harass. Do not pick Morph at level 1 - you need the 750
range positional escape from the very first creep wave. A Morphling without
Waveform at level 1 is just a squishy ranged creep with passive agility
steal.

## Pre-creep (0:00 - 0:15)
Move to mid lane river side to contest the bounty rune at 0:00. Morphling is
an agility carry with a poor HP pool, so do NOT fight for bounty against melee
strength heroes (Centaur, Timbersaw, Bristle). Grab the rune only if uncontested
or contested by squishy mages.

## First 90 seconds
Last-hit priority. Morphling has some of the best base damage among agility
carries (22-28) but low starting strength (21 base). You lose HP trades against
most mid heroes except other squishy mages. Target last-hit numbers: 25-30 by
3 minutes.

## Ability leveling (recommended, 7.41b)
Essence Shift is innate, so it never appears in this list — it's always on.
- Level 1: Waveform (Q)
- Level 2: Attribute Shift (R) - toggle to AGI for last-hit damage
- Level 3: Waveform
- Level 4: Adaptive Strike (W) - for ranged harass or stun utility

## DO NOT
- Do not harass with Waveform unless you can hit multiple units (wave + hero)
- Do not use mana on Adaptive Strike in the first 2 minutes - your mana pool
  is too small to sustain and you need mana for Waveform escape
- Do not shift to strength unless you are taking a gank - the agi shift is
  your damage
