# Morphling Trade Windows (3:00 - 6:30)

By minute 3, you should have:
- 2-3 levels in Waveform
- 1 level in Attribute Shift
- Wraith Band or Null Talisman in progress
- 25-40 last hits (adjusted for matchup)

## Trade-friendly matchups (you can initiate)
- vs Invoker (pre-level 6): Waveform through creeps, Adaptive Strike stun,
  shift to strength to survive return damage.
- vs Shadow Fiend: your HP pool is worse but your damage burst with ATK + AS
  is comparable; punish him when his Raze cooldowns are down.
- vs Tinker (pre-laser upgrade): Waveform hits through laser block and his
  HP pool is almost identical to yours.

## Avoid trades
- vs Lina: her burst is higher than yours AND she outranges you after level 6.
- vs Zeus: you cannot outtrade Lightning Bolt + Arc Lightning combo.
- vs QoP: her blink makes it impossible to punish her commits, and her
  Scream silence ruins your Shift-to-strength escape.

## Core trade pattern
1. Wait for enemy to commit mana on creep harass.
2. Waveform forward through the minion line to close the gap.
3. Right-click 2-3 times at max attack speed.
4. Adaptive Strike to disengage OR to continue pressure.
5. If taking return damage, Shift to strength immediately.

## Mana math for trades
Waveform + Adaptive Strike combo costs roughly 280 mana at level 4. A
Morphling without Bottle cannot pay that cost twice. If the enemy retreats
to their tower after your first trade, refill your bottle at the 4:00 rune
BEFORE trading again.
