# Defending Mid vs Rotations (3:00 - 6:30)

Morphling is a PRIORITY GANK TARGET for supports after minute 3 because:
1. Low starting HP pool (barely above 500 at level 5)
2. Squishy attribute distribution
3. Mana-dependent for escape (Waveform costs 150 / 160 / 170 / 180)

## Warning signs of incoming rotation
- Enemy offlane missing from the minimap for 20+ seconds
- Enemy support not visible at their tower for 15+ seconds
- Enemy courier spotted moving toward mid (often delivering stun wards or
  smoke to the ganker)
- Wards on your ramps or pulled camps suddenly going dark

## Defensive response
1. Back off to your tower immediately if 2+ enemies are missing.
2. Shift attributes to STRENGTH the moment you see a rotation. A full Str
   shift can add 200-300 HP in two seconds - enough to survive one stun
   chain.
3. Save Waveform for escape, not wave clear.
4. Consider burning an Ironwood Branch or Mango for emergency regen if you
   are already low.

## Do not
- Do not try to trade through a 3-man gank thinking you can Waveform out -
  supports will save their stuns for your escape window.
- Do not commit to a last hit in overextended position if enemies are
  missing. One creep is not worth a death.
- Do not TP to an ally's gank if your mid tower is being dove. Your tower
  is worth more than an assist.

## When the rotation arrives
- First stun lands: shift hard to strength (at least 70% Str).
- Second disable: Waveform directly over the treeline toward your tower -
  the tree path is unpredictable and wastes the ganker's second stun.
- If silenced: TP scroll is your only option; stand on the tree edge and
  channel.
