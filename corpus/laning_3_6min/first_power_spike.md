# Morphling First Power Spike (Level 6 + Yasha)

## Level 6 ultimate (Replicate)
Game-changing for Morphling. You can clone an ally or enemy. Do NOT use at
level 6 for wave clear unless you have mana to cast Waveform after. The
threat value of a level-6 Replicate alone changes the lane - the enemy mid
will play further back knowing you have a kill combo online.

## Target items by 6 minutes
- Power Treads (agility) - 1400g
- Wraith Band x2 or Wraith + Null - 1230g
- Total: approximately 2600g net worth by minute 6 is the baseline

## Target items by 10 minutes
- Yasha - 2050g (your first true power spike)
- Manta Style - 4600g (finishes around minute 14-16 in good games)

## Yasha timing target: 9:30 - 10:30
- Behind 11:00 = you are behind on schedule, need to farm harder
- Behind 13:00 = corpus expansion needed, investigate why (death? wrong item
  path? ganked off wave?)
- Behind 15:00 = the game is actively slipping; consider safer rotations
  instead of mid farm

## Why Yasha specifically
Yasha gives +16 Agility, 10 movement speed, 15 attack speed. The movement
speed and agility together keep you alive during the 10-15 minute window
when the enemy team will start grouping for tower pushes. Without Yasha
you are a 310 MS agility carry who dies to any 3-man rotation.
