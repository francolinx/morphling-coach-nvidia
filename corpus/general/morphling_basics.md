# Morphling Basics

## Role and archetype
Morphling is a position-1 agility-based hyper carry. He is weak in the first
0-6 minutes, becomes a lane bully with level 6 (Waveform + ult), and scales
into a late-game deleting machine with Ethereal Blade + Eblade combos. His
identity is "fragile in early, god in late." Treat the first 10 minutes as
survival farming; do not posture, do not overextend, do not chase kills off-
position. Morphling is not a fighter at level 3; he is a last-hit machine
with an escape that requires mana.

## Abilities

### Waveform (Q)
- Morphling turns liquid and surges forward, damaging enemies in a line.
- Primary use: mobility (escape and gap close) FIRST, damage SECOND.
- High mana cost (100-160). In lane, you almost never burn Waveform for
  damage - you save it as a Shadow-Blade-tier escape button.
- Goes through trees. Check escape paths before committing forward.

### Adaptive Strike - Agility (W)
- Ranged nuke that deals bonus damage and short stun/knockback that scales
  with your Agility-to-Strength ratio.
- At max Agi morph, this is a high-damage instant. At max Str morph, it is
  a long stun but low damage.
- Use in lane to secure ranged creep last-hits from distance or punish
  enemy for stepping on your wave.

### Adaptive Strike - Strength (E)
- Same ability, Strength variant. Lower damage, longer stun, knocks enemy
  back.
- Rarely maxed at position 1 - most builds max Agility Adaptive for damage.

### Attribute Shift (Shift Agility / Shift Strength)
- The defining mechanic. Convert Agility into Strength (HP) or Strength into
  Agility (damage + attack speed) at a rate per second.
- In lane: shift Agi -> Str to tank harass and survive dives. After the
  threat passes, shift back Str -> Agi to resume farming.
- Mid-fight: burst Str -> Agi to spike damage for a kill, or Agi -> Str to
  survive a chain stun.
- THIS IS THE HERO. If you are not shifting during fights, you are playing
  Morphling wrong.

### Morph / Replicate (R, level 6)
- Creates a controllable copy of an enemy hero with most of their abilities
  (no item actives in most cases).
- In lane, the ult's threat value alone changes the matchup - enemy must
  respect the possibility of a 2-hero counter.
- At level 6 + Waveform, you have a real kill threat for the first time.

## Talents (typical pick order)
- Level 10: +15 Agility (most standard) vs +175 Cast Range Adaptive.
- Level 15: +12% Magic Resist vs +25 Movement Speed - mag resist is
  stronger into magical mid lanes.
- Level 20: -4s Waveform cooldown is the standard - massive mobility
  uptime.
- Level 25: +4s Replicate duration vs +150 Adaptive damage - matchup
  dependent.

## Stat priorities
- Early: Agility > Strength > Intelligence. You need last-hit damage and
  attack speed. Max Agility Adaptive first.
- Skill build (typical): Q at 1, W max by 7, Attribute Shift as point
  filler, R at 6.
- Starting attribute: Agility is your friend.

## Morph mechanics (why shifting matters)
- Each point of Strength = ~20 HP. Shifting 10 Str in gives ~200 HP -
  that is "a creep wave of survival" mid-fight.
- Each point of Agility = ~1 damage + armor + attack speed. Shifting into
  full Agi before a kill swing can add 30-50 damage per hit.
- Shift speed scales with level. At level 6+, you can fully swap pools in
  a few seconds.

## Weaknesses
- Silences (Doom, Night Stalker's Crippling Fear, Drow Gust, Nyx Mana
  Burn) = dead Morphling. You cannot shift, cannot Waveform out.
- Burst mana drain (Nyx Mana Burn, Pugna Life Drain, Diffusal stacks) =
  dead Morphling.
- Hard lockdown with no BKB (Chrono, Black Hole, Reverse Polarity) = dead
  Morphling.
- Before level 6 with no Waveform mana, you are effectively a creep. Do
  not fight.
