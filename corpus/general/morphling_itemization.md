# Morphling Itemization

## Starting items (0:00)
- Tango (2 charges shared or 4 solo), Healing Salve, 2x Iron Branch,
  Slippers of Agility, Circlet (builds into Wraith Band).
- Alternative: Faerie Fire + extra branches if matchup is high-damage
  magical.
- Goal: have enough regen to survive 2-3 trades and one all-in from the
  offlane duo.

## Early game (0-10 min) - survival + lane dominance

### Wraith Band (priority 1, assemble by 3-4 min)
Cheap stats. +3 Agility matters on Morphling more than on almost any other
carry because Attribute Shift turns those stats directly into HP when
needed.

### Magic Stick / Magic Wand
If enemy has spammy low-cd abilities (Bristleback, Batrider, Zeus, Bane),
rush Stick by 1-2 min. Wand later. Charges + burst heal = extra HP pool
mid-trade.

### Bottle (mid lane standard)
You are mid lane Morphling. Bottle is non-negotiable unless you are off-
meta side lane. Rune control is your mana refill. Helps Waveform uptime.
Without bottle, you are one Waveform away from OOM.

### Power Treads (Agility)
Rush Treads by 6-8 min if not under dive pressure. Treads switching on
Morphling is not as critical as on Int heroes, but Treads on Agi is the
default for attack speed.

### Falcon Blade (optional)
Good flex pickup if mana pool is limiting Waveform casts. +10 mana regen
+ damage + HP. Disassembles into Morbid Mask.

## Transition item (8-10 min)

### Dragon Lance (~1900g, standard 8-10 min pickup)
A standard Morphling transition item in 7.41b. Provides cheap stats
(+13 Strength, +13 Agility) plus +130 attack range, which is enormous on
a hero whose base range is only 350. The range bonus lets you safely
last-hit and harass without overextending into gank range.

Build path notes:
- Disassembles cleanly: Ogre Club -> BKB later, Quarterstaff + recipe
  -> Yasha for Manta.
- Upgrades to Hurricane Pike for an active reposition (push enemy 470
  units away) — strong defensive tool against melee gap-closers.
- Slot in between Yasha and Ultimate Orb when you need more lane
  presence; otherwise prioritize Manta if you are even or ahead.

## Core items (10-25 min)

### Manta Style (core item #1, target by 15-18 min)
THE Morphling item. Illusions use your Agi-swapped form, dodge projectiles
(Venge stun, Lion Finger, Purge), break hexes. +26 Agility is massive. If
you buy nothing else, buy Manta.

### Ethereal Blade (core item #2, target by 22-28 min)
Active: ghosts the target, amplifies magical damage, and deals a damage burst
of 50 + 100% of the sum of the caster's attributes. THE Morphling combo: shift
all Str into Agi -> Eblade enemy -> Adaptive Strike -> right-click. Deletes
supports and squishy cores.

7.41 changes you must know:
- Cost is now ~4800g (Wraith Band removed from the recipe in 7.41).
- Magic resistance reduction is now 30% (was 40% pre-7.41).
- **Eblade is disabled against Debuff Immune targets — it FAILS vs BKB.**
  If the enemy support pops BKB you cannot ghost them, cannot amplify, and
  the active is wasted. Track BKB cooldowns before committing your combo.

### Linken's Sphere (situational but very common)
Blocks single-target lockdown (Doom, Hex, Duel, Lion Finger). If enemy has
one of these as win condition, Linkens > third core.

## Situational cores (pick based on matchup)

### Black King Bar (BKB)
Pick up if enemy has chain magical CC you cannot sphere (Tide, Enigma,
Void). Rarely skipped above 25 min unless the game is already won.

### Skadi (Eye of Skadi)
Stats + slow + extra HP/mana. Great into high-mobility cores (QoP, AM,
Puck). Good pickup if Manta + Eblade is already online and game is going
long.

### Butterfly
Evasion + Agility. Your late-game "I am now uncatchable and un-right-
clickable" item.

### Satanic
Lifesteal + Str active for unkillability. Pick up if you are low HP pool
and need a fight-sustaining item.

### Disperser
Newer item, upgrades from Diffusal. Purge on allies/self + mana break.
Situational but strong into heroes Morphling already hard-counters.

### Refresher Orb
**Patch 7.41 rework: Refresher Orb no longer refreshes item cooldowns.**
It only resets hero ability cooldowns. The old "double Eblade" and "double
Replicate" win conditions are no longer possible — do not plan around them.
Refresher is now a niche pickup for resetting Waveform / Adaptive Strike /
Morph in extreme late-game team fights, and is rarely worth a slot over
Butterfly, Skadi, or Satanic. Plan around a single Eblade combo and rely on
Manta + Waveform repositioning to reset fights instead.

## Anti-counter items
- Against silences: Eul's Scepter (dispels silence, also gives mana
  regen).
- Against illusion hate (AoE wipe): buy Manta anyway - the threat value
  matters more than the illusion surviving.
- Against invisibility: Sentries are a support job, but pick up Gem if
  you are the strongest core.

## What NOT to build
- Desolator: armor reduction is fine, but Morphling's value is magical
  burst + mobility, not armor shred. Skip.
- Daedalus: crit is wasted on Morphling because Eblade amp is magical;
  your right clicks are not your primary damage spike.
- Mjollnir before Manta/Eblade: stats are not in the right place for
  this hero's combo. (Dragon Lance is fine and recommended pre-Manta as
  a stat/range stepping-stone; upgrading it to Hurricane Pike comes
  AFTER Manta + Eblade are online.)

## Quick item timing checklist
- 4 min: Wraith Band + Bottle online.
- 8 min: Treads + regen pool (stick/wand).
- 8-10 min: Dragon Lance (standard transition pickup, ~1900g).
- 15 min: Manta done.
- 25 min: Eblade done (~4800g, remember it FAILS vs BKB).
- 30 min: Linkens or BKB depending on matchup.
