# Morphling Laning Overview (Mid, 0-15 min)

This is the evergreen overview of Morphling mid laning, preserved from the
pre-segmentation corpus as a fallback for queries that do not cleanly map
to one of the phase-specific files. For phase-specific detail, prefer the
files in `laning_0_3min/`, `laning_3_6min/`, and `laning_6_10min/`.

## The mental model
Morphling mid in the first 6 minutes is a last-hit machine with a panic
button (Waveform). You are NOT fighting. You are NOT trading. You are
watching the creep animations, clicking the right-click at the right
millisecond, and pressing Waveform only when your life is at risk. Every
last hit is worth ~40 gold plus the deny equivalent. Every death is worth
~300 gold handed to your opponent AND 30 seconds off the map.

## Pre-1 min setup
- Pull the camera back at 0:10. Scout the rune spawns for your bottle.
- Block the creeps if you are fast enough, but do not pull aggro -
  Morphling body-blocking against a ranged mid (SF, Puck, Lina) means
  eating free harass.
- Shift all stats into Strength at 0:30 if enemy mid is a magical-burst
  hero (Lina, Zeus, Shadow Shaman). Extra HP = extra survivability through
  early harass.

## Wave management
Default: hold the wave at your side of the river. Morphling farms best
in a slightly-pushed-toward-you wave because you can get ranged bottle
refill from rune spawns. Do NOT push the wave unless you want to rotate
for rune or stacked camp. Pushed wave = you are visible, exposed to ganks
from either side lane. At 2:00 and 4:00, runes spawn - clear your wave
~30 seconds before rune time, walk to the rune you have vision on, take
bottle charge or double-damage/haste for a kill attempt.

## When to harass (rare, conditional)
You have Wraith Band + you are at 100% HP + enemy mid is at 70% or below
+ enemy has no stun up + your Waveform is off cooldown as an escape = you
can walk up and right-click 2-3 times. Do NOT cast Waveform for damage in
lane unless it secures a kill. The mana cost means you cannot escape a
gank 5 seconds later. Max Adaptive Strike (W) by 7 - one 160 damage
ranged nuke is your harass tool, not Waveform.

## Level 6 power spike
At level 6, you get Morph/Replicate. Combined with a Waveform gap-close,
you now have a KILL THREAT in lane for the first time. Standard combo at
6: Waveform in -> Adaptive Strike (W) -> right-click 1-2 times -> Replicate
enemy if they try to flee. Only commit if you have >80% mana, your river is
warded (no gank incoming), and enemy is <50% HP. If the enemy has a TP
scroll up and your offlane / jungle is not pressured, abandon the kill
attempt.

## Shifting in lane
Default resting pose: 60% Agility, 40% Strength. Enough HP to tank one
magic nuke, enough damage to CS. Sudden incoming harass (you see enemy
winding up a spell): shift hard into Strength for ~2 seconds, eat the
nuke, shift back. When going for a kill at 6: shift 100% into Agility
~3 seconds before engagement so your hits deal max damage.

## Ganks and escape
River ward is your support's job. Your job is to check the minimap EVERY
2-3 last hits. If a lane goes missing and your support has not pinged,
assume gank. Pull back behind your ranged creep. Escape priority: Waveform
OVER the trees toward fountain > TP scroll > run and pray. Waveform mana
(100-160) should always be available. If you see a gank coming and you
are at <100 mana, that is a bottle-empty mistake from 30 seconds ago -
you should have refilled.

## Common lane opponents (overview)
- Shadow Fiend (SF): Easy matchup if you respect Raze range (400/600/800).
  SF has low starting HP. At level 3-4 with a rune, you can kill him with
  Waveform in + right clicks + Wraith Band stats.
- Puck: Medium matchup. Puck out-farms Morphling early, but Morphling
  out-scales by 20 min. Do not chase. Puck orb = free HP regen for them
  if you waste Waveform.

See `matchups/vs_burst_mages.md` for Lina, Zeus, QoP specific play.
See `matchups/vs_silence_heroes.md` for AA, Silencer, Orchid carriers.

## Red flags to call a support rotation
- You are below 400 gold at 6 min (under-farmed, need a rotation for gank
  or tower pressure).
- Enemy mid has a level advantage (2+ levels on you - something went
  wrong, get jungle camps).
- Enemy mid disappeared with a rune in their bottle - ping your sidelanes,
  they are being ganked.

## Rules of thumb
- Morphling does not win lane; Morphling survives lane and wins the game.
- Every Waveform cast in lane is a question: "Is my life worth more than
  this 100 mana?" If no, do not cast.
- Stats ARE items. 1 point into Attribute Shift saved for Str mid-fight
  can be worth 300 HP - more than most consumables.
