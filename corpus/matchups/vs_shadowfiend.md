# Morphling vs Shadow Fiend (Nevermore)

**Core principle:** Shadow Fiend (SF) is a damage-race matchup. Both
heroes scale on right-click damage and both spike around the same item
windows. The lane is decided by who hits their first power timing - your
Manta Style vs his Dagon or BKB - and by whose razes connect.

## Laning phase (0-6 min)

- SF's three razes (Shadowraze near/medium/far) hit a 250-radius area at
  fixed distances of 200/450/700 units in front of him. They are
  predictable - SF must turn to face you. Side-step perpendicular to his
  facing direction the moment you see the cast animation.
- SF gains permanent damage from souls (Necromastery): up to 90 bonus
  damage at 36 souls. Deny his creeps to keep his soul count low.
- Pre-shift to strength before walking into raze range to last-hit. The
  HP buffer absorbs one chained raze without forcing a TP.

## Item race (10-18 min)

- SF's classic damage spike is Shadow Blade or Eul's into Dagon 5
  (~14-16 min). His Dagon + Requiem of Souls combo at level 11 deals
  ~700+ magic damage in one second.
- Your Manta Style timing (target 14-16 min) MUST land before his Dagon
  charges go online, or every rotation you take will be a coin-flip.
- If SF builds Dagon, prioritize Hood of Defiance components or rush a
  Cloak (~550g) into Eternal Shroud. Magic resist beats raw HP against a
  Dagon SF.
- If SF builds Shadow Blade or Manta himself, your priority flips: you
  need DPS, not magic resist. Continue Manta-Yasha-Treads as standard.

## Mid-fight pattern

- SF's Requiem of Souls is a 700-radius PBAoE fear + magic damage (caps
  at ~600 dmg). 0.6s cast time - if you see the animation, Waveform OUT.
  Manta Style on cast blocks the fear (illusions eat one Requiem line).
- After he Requiems, SF has no escape and limited burst. That is your
  20-second window to all-in. Shift to agility, Adaptive Strike, right-
  click 4-5 times, Waveform out.

## When to all-in vs back off

- All-in if: you have Manta + Treads, SF has used Requiem in the last
  15 seconds, no enemy rotations visible.
- Back off if: SF has a stacked soul count (32+ souls visible on his
  model), no Manta yet, or his support has Force Staff for his escape.
