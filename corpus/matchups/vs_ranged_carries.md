# Morphling vs Ranged Carries (Luna, Drow, Viper)

**Core principle:** ranged carries are usually safe-laners you encounter
indirectly via rotations or side lane mid-game, not direct mid opponents.
The threat is their scaling - you MUST out-pace them to Manta or they will
simply outfarm you.

## vs Luna (offlane or safe lane)
- Lucent Beam deals burst magic + mini-stun, but she has no reliable gap
  close pre-level 6.
- If Luna rotates mid for a gank, shift to strength and tank one Beam;
  she cannot continue to burst without Moon Glaives procs.
- Luna scales faster than Morphling in theory, but in practice her Eclipse
  is PBAoE - it punishes squishy supports, not a Morphling who can
  Waveform to a safe side of a fight.

## vs Drow Ranger
- Gust silences Morphling for 5 seconds and slows you. This is the Drow
  threat - not her damage.
- If Drow is mid (rare), play at her tower edge. Her damage falls off at
  range.
- Mid-game, Drow's aura makes her team push structures fast. You need
  Manta online before Drow gets her 3-item spike (Manta + Hurricane +
  Treads), not after.

## vs Viper
- Viper's Poison Attack slow stacks and shreds Morphling's HP. She out-
  sustains and out-trades you in a straight stand-and-bang.
- Do not stand in her Nethertoxin for CS. Waveform in for a single last
  hit, Waveform out.
- Viper is one of the few ranged carries who can actually win mid against
  Morphling pre-6. Respect her trading range.

## General ranged carry defense
- Power Treads (agility) for the extra attack speed, so you can match
  their right-click DPS in a micro-trade.
- Eul's Scepter is a strong pivot if you are specifically fighting a
  hero whose damage comes from a DoT or slow (Viper, Drow Gust).
- Eye of Skadi late-game slow counters their kiting pattern, though this
  is post 25-minute territory.

## Positioning rule vs ranged carries
Your advantage is mobility. Their advantage is range. You lose at 700+
range and win at 300 range. Waveform closes that gap. Use it, right-click
3 times, Shift to agility burst, then Waveform out. Do not try to auto-
attack trade at range.
