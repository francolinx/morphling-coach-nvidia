# Morphling vs Lina (dedicated matchup)

**Core principle:** Lina is the canonical Morphling counter at low item
levels. Light Strike Array + Laser harass denies free last-hits, and
Laguna Blade at level 6 burst-equals Morphling's full HP pool. Survive
until your Manta + Linken's spike. See vs_burst_mages.md for general
burst-mage principles; this file covers Lina specifically.

## Laser harass mitigation (0-6 min)

- Laser deals 80/100/120/140 magic damage AND blinds 75% for 2.5s. Lina
  opens with Laser to deny CS, not to kill.
- Laser is instant-cast at 800 range - no dodge. Mitigation is positional:
  do not last-hit at the front of the wave when Lina has Laser mana.
- Pre-shift one Strength point before contested last-hits. The HP buffer
  means one Laser does not force a TP.
- Magic Stick is mandatory - Lina spams Laser, banking charges fast.

## Ult burst window (level 6+)

- Laguna Blade at level 6: 450 pure, 700 range, 0.65s cast. Level 6
  Morphling has ~920 HP. One Laser + one auto-attack → ~750 HP, then
  one Laguna kills outright.
- Level 25 talent + Aghanim's makes Laguna pure damage through BKB. At
  level 6-15 BKB DOES block Laguna - but you have no BKB at level 6.
- Solo-kill window is 6-14 min. After Manta (14-16 min), illusions
  absorb single-target spells and Lina's solo threat drops.

## Survival rules vs Lina ult

- Shift to STRENGTH before crossing the river or walking into a vision
  gap. 0.65s Laguna cast time is enough for her to react if you are
  stationary at low HP.
- Save Waveform for reactive escape, NOT engage. No Waveform on-screen
  vs Lina = one Laser-Laguna away from death.
- Manta dispels Light Strike Array stun on cast (LSA's 1.6s airtime is
  enough Manta time).

## Ethereal Blade as offensive answer

- In 7.41b, Eblade costs ~4800g and does NOT work vs BKB-active targets
  (docs/research/morphling_meta_2026-04-24.md section 4.2). Lina has no
  BKB before 25-30 min, so Eblade IS your burst answer 18-25 min.
- Eblade Lina → shift Str → Agi → Adaptive Strike (Agi) → 3 right-clicks.
  Ethereal amplifies magic damage taken by 30% (7.41 nerf from 40%),
  still one-shots Lina with Manta + Eblade + level 11+ Adaptive Strike.
