# Morphling vs Invoker

**Core principle:** Invoker is a skill-check matchup. His threat profile
shifts dramatically by build (Quas-Wex vs Quas-Exort vs Exort-Wex). You
must identify his orb build by 4:00 to know whether you are racing damage
or playing around CC. The ult-Aghanim's spike at 25-30 minutes is when
Invoker becomes uncatchable - your all-in window is BEFORE his Scepter.

## Identifying the build (look at the floating orbs)

- **Exort-heavy (red):** Sun Strike + Forge Spirit damage. Ranged poke.
- **Quas-heavy (blue):** Cold Snap + Ghost Walk + Tornado utility.
  Lockdown into teammate burst, not solo kill.
- **Wex-heavy (yellow):** EMP + Tornado + movement. Mid-game pusher.

## Dodging Sun Strike

- Sun Strike has a 1.7-second cast delay with a visible AoE marker on
  your screen (175 unit radius). It is dodgeable.
- At low HP after a trade you are a Sun Strike target. Move at least
  175 units when you see the marker. Do NOT stand still to last-hit if
  Invoker is off-screen and you are below 50% HP.
- Waveform is a hard dodge (untargetable mid-flight) but costs your
  escape. Use only if Sun Strike is part of a combo (EMP → Sun Strike).

## Quas variants and lane trading

- Against Exort, expect Forge Spirits at level 6 (two ranged units that
  reduce armor on hit). Kill the spirits BEFORE engaging Invoker -
  killing one spirit is one right-click.
- Against Quas, watch for Cold Snap. The proc-on-hit damage is the
  signal to disengage, not engage. Manta Style dispels Cold Snap on
  cast.
- EMP (Wex 4 + Quas 1) drains 350+ mana over 2.9 seconds. If you have
  no mana, you have no Waveform escape. Carry a Magic Stick into this
  matchup specifically for EMP recovery.

## All-in timing pre-Aghs

- Invoker's power spike is Aghanim's Scepter (~5000g, typical 25-30 min
  timing). Scepter unlocks Cataclysm (double Sun Strike with stun).
- Your all-in window: 14-22 minutes, after your Manta but before his
  Scepter. With Manta + Treads + a smoke gank, you can dive Invoker on
  his hard support and force a low HP TP.
- After his Aghs, do NOT solo dive Invoker. Cataclysm pins you while
  his team rotates.

## Item pivots

- Linken's Sphere blocks Cold Snap and Sun Strike. Pick up if Invoker
  is fed.
- Eul's is weaker here - Invoker has Tornado, so an Eul's race is a wash.
