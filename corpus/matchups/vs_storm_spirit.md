# Morphling vs Storm Spirit

**Core principle:** Storm Spirit is a mobility mirror - both heroes have
a movement ability (Ball Lightning vs Waveform), both scale on right-
click after item timing, and both die fast at low HP. The matchup is
decided by mana economy and by whose escape is on cooldown when the
fight starts.

## Mana burn potential

- Storm's kit runs on mana. Ball Lightning costs 15 + 12 per 100 units;
  a long jump drains 400+ mana.
- A mana-starved Storm is a stationary low-HP melee unit. Force him to
  spend mana early: zone in lane (forcing Static Remnant to clear creeps)
  or contest the 2:00 rune.
- Diffusal Blade (~2700g) is a strong situational pickup - active Purge
  slows Storm and burns 100 mana per stack. Two charges cancel a Ball
  Lightning escape mid-flight.

## Mid-lane trading

- Storm's Overload deals 50/75/100/125 magic damage on his next attack
  after casting any spell. He will lead with Static Remnant (creates a
  stationary clone) - the Remnant proc gives him an Overloaded right-
  click for free.
- Pre-shift to strength before walking past a placed Static Remnant.
  Better still: do not walk past Remnants. Detour around them.
- Storm's Electric Vortex (Quas-style pull) pulls you toward him for
  1.5/2/2.5/3 seconds. It is a single-target stun-pull. Manta Style
  dispels Vortex on cast.

## Eul's vs Linken's choice

- **Eul's Scepter (~2725g):** dispels Storm's Vortex on self-cast,
  gives 2.5s invulnerability that breaks line of sight. Strong if Storm
  is initiating with Vortex into team follow-up. Mana regen helps
  Morphling sustain Waveform spam.
- **Linken's Sphere (~4900g):** blocks Vortex outright (one charge per
  20 seconds). Stats are stronger for Morphling. Strong if Storm is the
  primary solo-kill threat AND has no AoE follow-up.
- **Pick Eul's if:** Storm has CC teammates (Tide, Mag, ES). Pick
  Linken's if: Storm is solo-killing and his team lacks chain-CC.

## Late-game (post-Manta)

- Storm's late-game Bloodstone + Aghs build wins 1v1 at equal net worth.
  Be 4000+ gold ahead by 25:00 or Storm wins.
- BKB priority shifts up if Storm has Aghs (Static Remnant cooldown
  reduction makes the lane unsafe without BKB).
- Hurricane Pike is excellent here - the thrust opens 600 units, breaking
  Vortex range and buying time to right-click as Storm closes.
