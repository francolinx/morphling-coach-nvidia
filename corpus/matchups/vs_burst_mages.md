# Morphling vs Burst Mages (Lina, Zeus, QoP)

**Core principle:** burst mages out-damage Morphling's early HP pool. You
cannot win a pure damage race in the first 8 minutes against these heroes.

## vs Lina
- Her Laguna Blade at level 6 deals 450 pure damage. Your level 6 HP is ~920.
- Build Null Talisman > Wraith Band in her matchup (magic resist > damage).
- Shift to STRENGTH before engaging - the HP swing saves you from Laguna.
- Do not chase Lina past the midline - her combo burst when cornered kills
  you.

## vs Zeus
- Arc Lightning + Lightning Bolt combo at level 7 does ~500 magic damage.
- Zeus has vision on you permanently via Nimbus / Thundergod's Wrath sight.
- Play far forward in lane to force Zeus to expend mana on creep clear.
- Push him off the rune at 2:00 - a mana-poor Zeus is a neutralized Zeus.

## vs QoP
- Blink Dagger range (1200) means she initiates, not you.
- Scream of Pain + Shadow Strike = ~400 magic damage in one combo.
- Save Waveform for reactive escape, not initiation.
- Shift to strength before she blinks - if you see her blink animation,
  shift immediately.

## General defense
- Magic Stick is high value when laning against these heroes (charges
  quickly from their ability spam).
- Consider Pipe of Insight in team fights post-laning.
- Manta Style dispels Shadow Strike's DoT, but does NOT dispel an incoming
  Laguna Blade or Finger-level burst - the Manta must already be cast.
