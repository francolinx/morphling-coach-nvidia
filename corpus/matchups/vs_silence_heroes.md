# Morphling vs Silence Heroes (Ancient Apparition, Silencer, Orchid)

**Core principle:** silence disables Morphling's survival toolkit (Waveform,
Adaptive Strike, Shift). A silenced Morphling with no escape dies in 1-2
seconds.

## vs Ancient Apparition (AA)
- Ice Blast at any level PERMANENTLY prevents HP regeneration and caps HP
  for the duration. If AA lands Ice Blast on you, you cannot Shift to
  strength to tank - the strength HP pool counts as regen and is capped.
- AA's Chilling Touch adds magic damage to right clicks - stacks with
  Zeus/Lina teammates.
- Play safer than normal in AA matchup. Do not trade.

## vs Silencer
- Global Silence at level 6 disables your entire kit for 4-6 seconds.
- Time your Waveform escape BEFORE Global Silence lands (there is a
  distinctive cast sound - learn it).
- After Global Silence is used, you have a ~70 second window of safety
  before his next cast. Use that window to farm or push.
- Curse of the Silent is a DoT+silence that Manta Style dispels.

## vs Orchid carriers (Shadow Fiend, Mirana, Bounty Hunter)
- Orchid procs on your Waveform and locks you out of escape.
- BKB (Black King Bar) is your answer post-Manta - consider pivoting to
  earlier BKB if multiple Orchid threats are on the enemy team.
- In laning, avoid extended trades - use Waveform in-and-out, not engage-trade.

## Response items
- Mango Tree and Magic Stick for quick burst heal through silence.
- Eul's Scepter as a pivot item - dispels silences on self, plus 2.5s
  invulnerability. A strong pickup if two silence threats are on the
  enemy team.
- Manta Style remains highest priority - dispels single-target silences on
  cast (the cast itself is silence-immune).
