# Morphling vs Doom

**Core principle:** Doom is Morphling's hardest counter. His ultimate is a
12-16 second mute that disables Waveform, Adaptive Strike, AND Attribute
Shift simultaneously. A Doomed Morphling cannot escape, cannot shift HP,
cannot reposition - he simply dies. The 2026-04-24 deep-research review
flagged this as the most critical missing matchup in the corpus (see
docs/research/morphling_meta_2026-04-24.md, section 5).

## Why Doom breaks Morphling's kit

- Doom is NOT a silence and NOT a hex - it is a custom debuff that mutes
  spells AND items AND passives. Manta Style cannot dispel Doom. BKB does
  not block Doom in 7.41b.
- Once Doomed, your Strength shift is locked at whatever value you had when
  the debuff applied - stuck at low HP if you were in agility mode.
- Scorched Earth lets Doom out-walk you on flat ground.

## Laning phase (0-10 min)

- Doom is usually offlane, not mid - you encounter him via rotations or
  side lane skirmishes around the 6-min mark.
- Track his level. Doom (ultimate) is available at level 6 with a 110/100/90
  second cooldown. After he uses it on a teammate, you have a ~90-second
  window where mid-air aggression is safe.
- Devour at level 1-3 gives Doom creep abilities (typically Centaur stomp
  or Satyr purge). Stay out of stomp range when he has a charge banked.

## Item priority shift

- **BKB before Manta** in any game with Doom on the enemy team. Manta does
  not save you from being Doomed mid-fight; BKB at minimum prevents the
  follow-up from his teammates while Doom ticks.
- **Lotus Orb (~3850g)** reflects Doom back on the caster. Time the Lotus
  active when you see Doom move into 550 cast range. Lotus has a 90-second
  cooldown - same order of magnitude as Doom's ult, so one Lotus = one
  reflected Doom per fight.
- **Eul's Scepter** is a fallback: cast on self the instant you see Doom's
  cast animation (1.0s cast point). The 2.5s invulnerability eats the cast.
  This is tight timing and only works if you react to the animation start.

## Fighting pattern

Do not initiate where Doom can reach you with Blink Dagger. If your team
can burn Doom's ultimate on a frontliner first, the remaining 90 seconds
is your window to play forward and split-push.
