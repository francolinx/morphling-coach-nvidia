# Manta Style Timing (6:30 - 10:00)

**Manta is THE Morphling item.** Everything before Manta is setup.

## Why Manta
- Dispels silences and debuffs (critical against AA, Lion, Shadow Demon)
- Creates two illusions that duplicate Morphling's current stats - including
  Shift-boosted strength
- Enables split-push and escape patterns unique to Morphling: Waveform in,
  Manta split, run three different directions

## Target Manta timing: 14-16 minutes
In practice this means you need to be actively transitioning into the Ultimate
Orb purchase by minute 10. If you are still buying Yasha components past 10:00
you are off-pace.

## Component path
1. Yasha (2050g total) - complete by 10:00
2. Ultimate Orb (2100g) - buy immediately after Yasha
3. Manta recipe (700g) - combine as soon as you can afford it

## If behind on Manta
- Past 16:00 without Manta = actively bad, need to recover
- Past 18:00 = the game is likely lost from the Morphling perspective
- Past 20:00 = you failed the fundamental timing, review the replay

## Common Manta timing failures
- Dying more than 2x in laning phase (each death = ~600g lost)
- Going for wrong intermediate items (Bottle-Magic-Stick priority over
  Yasha pieces)
- Jungle stacking instead of last-hitting (low value for early Morphling -
  you do not clear camps fast enough to justify the trip yet)
- Missing the 4:00 or 6:00 bounty runes (120g each, plus the 2:00 rune
  opportunity cost of a bottle refill you did not get)

## What to do the minute Manta is in your inventory
Test it once on a creep wave to confirm it works and your illusions are
positioned right. Then take your first objective push with your team -
Manta is a fighting and pushing item, not a passive stat stick.
