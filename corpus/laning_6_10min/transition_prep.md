# Transition Prep (6:30 - 10:00)

The window 6:30-10:00 is where Morphling stops being a laner and starts
being a positional mid-game carry. You are transitioning from "survive and
CS" to "farm efficiently and apply pressure." Execute these transitions
deliberately - do not coast.

## Farm pattern change
Laning farm pattern: stand at mid, last-hit creeps. Post-laning farm pattern:
use a three-part rotation of mid wave -> adjacent jungle camp -> mid wave.
Each cycle takes roughly 90 seconds and nets more gold per minute than
staying on one wave.

## Ward vision
By 7:00 your team should have vision on both of your side-jungle entrances
and one deep ward near the enemy's hard camp. If not, ping your supports
and buy an observer ward yourself. Morphling with no vision is a Morphling
who dies to a smoke gank at 8:30.

## Buyback and tp discipline
- Always have a TP scroll by 7:00. Always.
- By minute 10 you should have accumulated enough reliable gold for one
  buyback at your current net worth (~550g at level 8-9).
- Do not spend your buyback gold on items. That is the stash for when you
  get caught out and the enemy pushes a tower you need to defend.

## Team contribution in this window
- You are not yet strong enough to carry a 5v5 fight. Do not initiate.
- You CAN contribute to a support save on the flanks by Waveforming in,
  Adaptive Strike stun, and peeling off. Keep this commitment short.
- Smoke ganks on the enemy mid or offlane are high-value here because you
  have an ult and a Wraith Band, but the enemy often still lacks one or
  both.

## Itemization micro-decisions
- If you are ahead: rush Ultimate Orb after Yasha for the fastest possible
  Manta.
- If you are even: standard path, finish Power Treads if you did not
  already.
- If you are behind: buy Magic Wand now (you skipped it in lane, and the
  charges save lives) before committing to Ultimate Orb.

## Dragon Lance: standard 8-10 min transition pickup (7.41b)
Dragon Lance (~1900g) is the standard transition item for Morphling in this
window. The +130 attack range is huge on a 350-base-range hero — it lets
you last-hit, harass, and farm jungle camps from outside gap-close range.
Stat distribution (+13 Str / +13 Agi) is exactly what Morphling wants:
Agi for damage and HP via Attribute Shift, Str for an immediate HP buffer
without shifting.

When to slot it:
- Standard line: Yasha (~10:00) -> Dragon Lance (~10-12:00) -> Ultimate Orb
  -> Manta. Costs ~30-60s of Manta delay but you survive the window.
- Skip Dragon Lance only if you are clearly ahead and can rush Manta safely.
- Disassembly path later: Ogre Club -> BKB; Quarterstaff + recipe -> Yasha
  if you somehow built it before Yasha.
- Upgrades to Hurricane Pike post-Manta for the active push/pull reposition.
