# First Tower Pressure (6:30 - 10:00)

Morphling is NOT a tower-pushing hero. You have no summons, no cleave, no
siege bonus. The T1 mid tower falls when it falls - usually around minute 9-11
if one side is winning lane, later if the lane is even. Your job in this
window is to decide whether to press the tower or pivot to farming.

## When to press the mid T1
Press only if all three conditions are true:
1. You have Yasha or are within 300g of finishing it.
2. Your support has rotated to mid and can tank one tower shot.
3. The enemy mid has TP cooldown (recently used) or is dead.

If any of these is false, do not commit. A half-hearted tower dive loses the
tower, gives the enemy gold, and costs you farm time.

## When to NOT press the tower
- You are behind on Yasha timing.
- The enemy has a blink initiator off cooldown (Centaur Stampede, Slardar
  Slithereen Crush, Tidehunter Ravage).
- You have less than 75% mana. Tower pressure without Waveform escape is a
  suicide mission.
- The enemy mid has a teleport scroll in inventory (check the scoreboard).

## How to push the T1 safely
- Hit the tower with the creep wave, not alone. Morphling's solo DPS on
  structures is mediocre.
- Between tower hits, move back to a ward-lit position. Do not stand in
  fog checking the HP bar.
- If the wave pushes into tower range, Waveform out at 30% HP. Do not
  tank tower shots to deny the deny.

## After the T1 falls
- Pull a medium or large jungle camp on your way back.
- Rotate to a sidelane to contest a second T1 if the map allows.
- Consider placing a deep ward on the enemy ancient stacks camp for future
  farm paths.
