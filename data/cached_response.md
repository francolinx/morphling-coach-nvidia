## Item Timing

The single most impactful miss is **Manta Style — it never came online (timing: null).** For Morphling, Manta is not a luxury item; it is the spike that converts you from a fragile farmer into a fight-relevant core, and it is your dispel answer to Viper's Nethertoxin slow and Warlock/Lion's lockdown. Going into the 18:40 Roshan fight without Manta *and* without BKB meant you had neither the mobility to reposition nor the immunity to survive being focused. Magic Wand (4:00) and Wraith Band (3:00) were fine early pickups, but the game was decided by the **purple item that was still missing 18 minutes in**.

## Team Fight Execution

The 16:20 fight (lost, your line 0/1/1) is the clearest example of the pattern: you **engaged before Manta**. Morphling's entire team-fight identity is built on the Manta + Ethereal Blade / Morph tempo — without that power spike you have no burst, no escape, and no reason to be in melee range. You contributed one assist and one death to a fight you had no business being in front for. Until your core spike is online, your value is in farming flanks and split-pushing, not standing on the front line.

## Three Practice Goals

1. **Hit 45+ last hits at 10:00 in your next three Morphling games.** You are at 31 — close the gap by treating Waveform as a CS-and-deny tool in the Viper lane, not an aggression button.
2. **Reduce laning-phase deaths to 0–1.** Both of your sub-7-minute deaths came from overextension (no-escape mid, solo deep ward). Keep Waveform charged as an escape and let supports buy the map vision.
3. **Reach a Manta Style timing of 16:00 or earlier, and never take a Roshan or 5v5 fight without it (plus BKB versus this lineup).** Your two biggest deaths were both "no spike, focused first" — fix the spike and the deaths disappear.
